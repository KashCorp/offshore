ABOUT OFFSHORE

There are huge, untapped reserves of oil and gas beneath the world’s oceans, 90 billion barrels in the Arctic alone. As conventional sources of oil decline, everyone from international oil majors to governments wants in on the game.

Yet this new oil frontier comes with incredible risk-- financial, environmental and social. Opponents passionately believe that it is not a case of “if” another oil spill happens but when.

OFFSHORE, invites viewers on a complex journey through this new frontier where a profound struggle is evolving with huge stakes for the planet’s future.

Our first chapter begins with the story of the Deepwater Horizon disaster.
